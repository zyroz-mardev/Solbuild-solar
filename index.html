Technology

Inside Every Solbuild Solar Panel
Layer by Layer

Every component is precision-engineered for maximum output and longevity in Indian climate conditions.

01
🌞

Anti-Reflective Tempered Glass

3.2mm thick tempered solar glass with anti-reflective coating captures every photon. Built to withstand hail, dust storms and extreme Kanpur summer heat for 25+ years.

02
⚡

Monocrystalline Silicon Cells

High-efficiency monocrystalline cells deliver 20% plus conversion efficiency. Each cell is precision-cut for uniform performance and maximum power output per square metre.

03
🖥

EVA Encapsulant Layer

Ethylene Vinyl Acetate encapsulation protects the photovoltaic cells from moisture, UV degradation and mechanical damage while maintaining optical clarity.

04
🔗

Copper Busbar Grid

Multi-busbar copper grid design minimises electrical resistance and current path length. More busbars means lower power loss and better performance in partial shading.

05
🔌

Weatherproof Junction Box

IP67-rated junction box with bypass diodes prevents hot spots and protects against reverse current. Engineered specifically for Indian heat, humidity and monsoon conditions.

06
🏗

Anodized Aluminum Frame

Corrosion-resistant anodized aluminum frame provides structural rigidity. Load-tested to withstand wind speeds of 130 km/h and snow loads relevant for northern India.
